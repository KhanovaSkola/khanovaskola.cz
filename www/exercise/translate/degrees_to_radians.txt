99.1 	 Degrees to radians

99.2 	 Convert the angle <var>ANGLE.deg</var>&deg; to radians.

99.3 	 There are <code>360</code> degrees in a circle. There
                        are also <code>2\pi</code> radians in a circle.

99.4 	 In other words, there are <code>\pi</code> radians per
                        <code>180</code> degrees. So we can convert from
                        degrees to radians by multiplying by <code>\pi</code>,
                        and dividing by <code>180^\circ</code>.
